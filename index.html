Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app free to use for NGOs?
Yes, ReliefSync is completely free for registered NGOs and volunteer groups. We believe in saving lives over profits.
How does the Offline Mode work?
The app stores data locally on your device when there is no internet. Once you reconnect to a network, it automatically syncs all reports to our central dashboard.
Can I integrate this with my existing systems?
Absolutely. We provide a robust API that allows you to feed data from your existing management systems into ReliefSync for unified command.
